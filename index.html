Agumbe

Agumbe is a scenic village in Karnataka's Western Ghats, known for its heavy rainfall, lush rainforests, and breathtaking sunsets. It is a biodiversity hotspot and home to the rare King Cobra, attracting nature lovers and trekkers.

Abbey Falls

Abbey Falls is a beautiful waterfall near Madikeri in Karnataka’s Coorg district. Surrounded by coffee and spice plantations, it cascades from a height of 70 feet, offering a scenic view, especially during the monsoon. A hanging bridge nearby provides a great vantage point, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Karwar

Karwar is a coastal town in Karnataka, known for its pristine beaches, scenic landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It lies along the Arabian Sea and is a gateway to Goa. Karwar is famous for its tranquil Devbagh Beach, seafood, and the nearby Kali River, making it a popular destination for nature and water sports enthusiasts.

Badami

Badami is a historic town in Karnataka, known for its stunning rock-cut cave temples and red sandstone cliffs. Once the capital of the Chalukya dynasty, it features ancient carvings, temples, and a picturesque reservoir, making it a significant site for history and architecture enthusiasts.

Kodachadri Trek

Kodachadri Trek is a popular trekking route in Karnataka, leading to the Kodachadri peak in the Western Ghats. Known for its lush forests, waterfalls, and breathtaking views, the trek takes adventurers through dense vegetation and steep trails, culminating in stunning vistas from the summit. The peak is also home to the ancient Mookambika Temple, adding spiritual significance to the journey.

Virupaksha Temple

Virupaksha Temple in Hampi is a historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its intricate architecture and cultural significance.

The Stone Chariot

The Stone Chariot in Hampi is an iconic granite structure resembling a chariot, located in the Vittala Temple complex, known for its detailed carvings and architectural brilliance.

River Tungabhadra

River Tungabhadra is a major river in southern India, flowing through Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh, known for its historical and cultural significance.

Jog Falls

Jog Falls is India's second-highest plunge waterfall, located in Karnataka, known for its breathtaking four cascades: Raja, Rani, Roarer, and Rocket.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace is a grand royal residence in Karnataka, known for its stunning architecture and cultural significance.

Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a historic palace in Bengaluru, inspired by Tudor architecture, known for its elegant interiors and royal heritage.

Someshwar Beach

Someshwar Beach is a serene beach near Mangaluru, known for its golden sands, rocky shores, and the ancient Someshwara Temple.

Bharachukki Waterfalls

Bharachukki Waterfalls is a stunning segmented waterfall in Karnataka’s Chamarajanagar district, known for its wide cascade and scenic beauty.

Chennakesava Temple

Chennakesava Temple is a historic Hoysala-era temple in Karnataka, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, renowned for its intricate carvings and architectural beauty.
